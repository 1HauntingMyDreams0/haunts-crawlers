Return & Refund Policy

General Policy

All sales are considered final once completed. Due to the nature of live animals and perishable or fragile items, we do not accept returns or exchanges.

We do not resell returned animals or used biological materials under any circumstances.

Local Pickup Orders

All orders are pickup only and must be collected at the agreed location and time.

  • Please inspect your order at the time of pickup.
  • Once an order has been accepted during pickup, it is considered final.
  • We are not responsible for issues reported after the order has left our possession.

If you are unable to make your pickup time, please contact us in advance to reschedule.

Live Animals & Biological Products

All live animals (including isopods and other invertebrates) are sold as-is at the time of pickup.

Because we cannot control conditions after the animal leaves our care, we do not offer refunds or returns for live animals once they have been taken.

Order Accuracy Issues

If you receive the wrong item or there is an issue with your order at pickup, please notify us immediately so we can correct it on the spot.

We cannot make changes or refunds after pickup has been completed.

Health & Care Responsibility

We take care to provide healthy animals and properly prepared materials. However, the buyer is responsible for providing appropriate care, habitat, and conditions after pickup.

We are always happy to answer care questions before and after purchase.

Contact

If you have any concerns about your order, please contact us as soon as possible so we can help resolve the issue.
